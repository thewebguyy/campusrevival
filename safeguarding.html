Safeguarding & Ethics

Commitment to Safety

The Campus Revival Movement (CRM) is committed to protecting all users, particularly students and young people, from harm and exploitation. We adhere to the UK Data Protection Act 2018 and GDPR standards.

Digital Safety & Privacy

  • Anonymity: Real-time adoptions only show first names to maintain privacy.
  • Verification: High-level campus leaders are verified via institutional email to ensure authentic representation.
  • Moderation: All journal entries and prayer requests are subject to automated and human moderation.

Ethical Outreach

We believe in respectful, consensual spiritual engagement. Our advocates are encouraged to:

  • Respect institutional rules and campus guidelines.
  • Foster inclusive environments for all faiths and backgrounds.
  • Maintain healthy boundaries in virtual and physical prayer walking.

Report a Concern

If you encounter inappropriate behavior or have concerns about a campus board, please contact our Safeguarding Officer immediately.
